About Us

We are Edibubble!

Edibubble, a visionary social enterprise, emerged from a student's profound concern for our planet's future. The journey commenced when Ryan Maudgalya delved into the far-reaching consequences of plastic water bottles as part of an assessment. His research highlighted the dire need for sustainable solutions, leading him to envision a world free from the shackles of plastic pollution. This seed of an idea germinated into a collective endeavor that birthed Edibubble.

Originally conceived as a venture to create edible water bottles, the concept evolved through thoughtful consideration. Recognizing the readily available water resources at NIST, the team redirected their innovation towards a new horizonâ€”edible juice bottles. Inspired by the pioneering work of Ooho water, which ingeniously encapsulated water within edible spheres, Edibubble embarked on a mission to reshape the way we consume beverages.

At its core, Edibubble is driven by a resolute mission: combating the pervasive pollution crisis. An astonishing 400 million tonnes of non-biodegradable plastic are discarded annually, wreaking havoc on our environment. Edibubble's mission is to counteract this menace by researching and crafting bio-friendly alternatives to conventional packaging. By offering innovative solutions, Edibubble aims to rejuvenate the delicate balance of our planet's ecosystem.

This is Us!

The vision that propels Edibubble is one of global transformation. The enterprise aspires to spearhead a movement that transcends geographical boundaries, initiating a paradigm shift in packaging practices. Anchored in Thailand, Edibubble envisions a future where non-biodegradable packaging is relegated to history, supplanted by bio-friendly alternatives embraced worldwide.

Guided by unwavering values, Edibubble's approach is rooted in innovation and ethical integrity. The team places paramount importance on creating packaging alternatives that are both safe and sustainable. Their commitment extends beyond product innovation to encompass an ethical framework that prioritizes the well-being of all stakeholders involved.

In the tapestry of sustainable entrepreneurship, Edibubble emerges as a beacon of ingenuity and conscious action. Armed with an indomitable spirit, Edibubble pioneers a path toward a greener, cleaner future, one edible juice bottle at a time.
